- The "Total Team Turnover" is the sum of all turnover amounts generated by your Level 1 referrals.

What is Team Revenue?”

Team Turnover refers to the total business generated from your first level referrals. In the context of a referral program like the one for Quant Guard, it means the collective turnover or revenue generated by the individuals you directly refer to the program.

In simpler terms, it's the combined sales, transactions, or activities generated by the people you directly bring into the program. Team turnover is a key metric in referral programs as it reflects not only your own efforts but also the effectiveness of your ability to recruit and motivate others to participate and contribute to the program's success.
